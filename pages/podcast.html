Messy but Trying

A podcast by Amber Hilton about navigating librarianship, graduate school, and creative work — honoring the messy process of trying to build more just futures.

About the Podcast

Messy but Trying is a space for reflection, storytelling, and honest conversations about life in and around libraries. Episodes often touch on public librarianship, community care, Black information futures, and the realities of working with and against technology.

Listeners can expect a mix of solo reflections, guest conversations, and in-progress ideas.
